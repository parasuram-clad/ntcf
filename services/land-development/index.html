Land Development and Pavement Construction

Laying the groundwork for smarter, safer connectivity

At NTC Infra, we specialize in comprehensive roadway construction and land development services, essential for enabling industrial growth, township planning, and public infrastructure. From highways and approach roads to site grading and drainage systems, we deliver engineered solutions that are built to last.

Our Services Include

Earthwork & Land Grading

Cutting, filling, and compaction to prepare raw land for development, ensuring proper slope, stability, and readiness for construction.

Plot Demarcation & Site Infrastructure

Compound walls, street lighting bases, water lines, and underground duct provisions for residential, commercial, or industrial layouts.
