Piping and Electro-Mechanical Works

Precision Engineering for Industrial Systems & Infrastructure

At NTC Infra, we provide integrated electro-mechanical works that form the backbone of industrial and infrastructure projects. From electrical systems and HVAC to pumping stations and mechanical installations, our team delivers turnkey solutions with safety, efficiency, and long-term reliability at the core.

Our Electro-Mechanical Services Include

Electrical Installations

HT/LT panels, transformers, cabling, switchgear, earthing, and internal/external lighting for industrial, commercial, and utility infrastructure.

Mechanical Equipment Installation

Setup and alignment of industrial machinery, compressors, blowers, motors, and mechanical piping systems.

Pumping Systems & Water Handling Units

Installation of pumping stations for water supply, sewage, or effluent systems, including control panels and automation integration.

HVAC & Ventilation Systems

Design and execution of ventilation, exhaust, and cooling systems for factories, treatment plants, and utility buildings.

Instrumentation & Automation Support

Integration of SCADA, PLC panels, sensors, and remote monitoring systems for smarter control of operations.
