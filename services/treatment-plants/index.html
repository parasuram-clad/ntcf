Water and Wastewater Treatment Plants

Sustainable Solutions for Water, Wastewater, and Effluent Treatment

NTC Infra offers expert design and engineering services for water, wastewater, and effluent treatment plants, enabling industries and public utilities to manage resources efficiently and comply with environmental standards. Our approach blends engineering precision with sustainable practices to deliver reliable treatment infrastructure tailored to each client’s needs.

Water Treatment Plants (WTPs)

Design and layout of systems for purification, filtration, and distribution of potable and process water.

Sewage Treatment Plants (STPs)

Turnkey engineering solutions for domestic and municipal sewage treatment using technologies like SBR, MBBR, and MBR.

Effluent Treatment Plants (ETPs)

Design of ETPs for industrial sectors such as textiles, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing units.

Sludge Management & Reuse Systems

Sludge dewatering, composting, and recycling plans integrated into plant design for sustainable operation.

What Sets NTC Infra Apart?

Customized Designs

Each plant is engineered based on specific influent characteristics, capacity needs, and regulatory requirements.

Process Engineering Expertise

Collaboration with process consultants to ensure technically sound and economically viable systems.

End-to-End Planning

From concept design and feasibility to detailed engineering drawings, BOQs, and site layout.

Regulatory Compliance

Designs that comply with CPCB, PCB, and environmental clearance norms to ensure smooth approvals.

Engineering Infrastructure that Protects the Environment

Whether you're planning a new facility or upgrading an existing one, NTC Infra offers the design and engineering capabilities to build treatment plants that perform efficiently and responsibly.
