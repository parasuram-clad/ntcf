Piling, Foundation and Heavy Civil Works

Building strong foundations for public and industrial growth

At NTC Infra, we specialize in delivering high-quality civil infrastructure solutions that support the backbone of public services and industrial development. With a proven track record in executing complex infrastructure projects, we work with government bodies, public sector undertakings, and industrial clients to construct reliable, sustainable, and future-ready civil assets.

Our Civil Infrastructure Capabilities Include

Industrial Site Development

Land grading, drainage, boundary walls, internal roads, utility access, and complete civil works for industrial zones and factories.

Public Utility Infrastructure

Construction of water treatment plants, stormwater drainage systems, sewage networks, and other essential civil utilities.

Roads and Bridges

Earthwork, pavement construction, culverts, retaining structures, and small to mid-scale bridges for urban and rural development.

Industrial Buildings & Structures

Foundations, substructures, RCC frames, and pre-engineered building supports for manufacturing plants, warehouses, and logistic parks.

Drainage & Stormwater Management

Execution of canal works, concrete drains, culverts, and related infrastructure to ensure effective water flow and flood prevention.
