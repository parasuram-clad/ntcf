Piling, Foundation and Heavy Civil Works

Building strong foundations for public and industrial growth

At NTC Infra, we specialize in piling, foundation, and heavy civil works that create the backbone of safe, resilient, and long-lasting infrastructure. From deep foundation systems to complex structural supports, our expertise ensures stability even in the most challenging geotechnical conditions.

Our Piling, Foundation and Heavy Civil Works Capabilities Include

Piling Solutions

Execution of driven cast-in-situ, bored cast-in-situ, micro piles, and sheet piling systems tailored to soil profiles and structural demands.

Foundation Engineering

Design and construction of shallow and deep foundations including raft, pile caps, footings, and ground improvement systems.

Retaining & Protective Structures

Development of retaining walls, diaphragm walls, cofferdams, and soil stabilization systems for secure load-bearing support.

Heavy Civil & Concrete Works

Execution of bridges, culverts, industrial basements, utility tunnels, and other critical RCC structures with precision engineering.

Ground Engineering & Soil Stabilization

Ground improvement, grouting, dewatering, slope protection, and embankment stabilization for difficult terrains.

Geotechnical Expertise
