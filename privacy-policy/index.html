Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01-07-2025

NTC Infra (legally NTC Infrastructure and Engineering Pvt Ltd) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may provide through our website www.ntcinfra.com. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Company Name (if applicable)
  • Location or Address (optional)
  • Project-related information
  • Other voluntarily submitted information via forms

We also collect non-personal data like browser type, device, IP address, and pages visited (via cookies and analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries or requests
  • To provide quotes or service information
  • To improve our website and services
  • To communicate updates or promotions (opt-in only)
  • To ensure site security and prevent misuse

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your information. We may share it only with:

  • Internal team members
  • Trusted third-party service providers with contracts
  • Law enforcement if required by law

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and analytics to understand visitor behavior. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute safety online.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or request your data
  • Correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent (if applicable)
  • Contact us for privacy concerns

7. Third-Party Links

We may link to external websites. Their privacy practices are separate from ours.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a new update date.
