Warehouse Development

Customized, scalable warehousing solutions for industrial and commercial needs

At NTC Infra, we design and build high-performance warehouse infrastructure that supports the evolving needs of logistics, manufacturing, and retail industries. Whether it's a storage facility, distribution center, or an integrated logistics park, our team delivers turnkey warehouse solutions—from land development to structural execution.

Our Warehouse Development Services Include

Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) Warehouses

Fast-track construction of steel-structured warehouses with flexible design, high durability, and efficient space utilization.

RCC & Hybrid Structures

Construction of conventional RCC warehouses or hybrid designs that balance cost and functionality.

Cold Storage Warehouses

Temperature-controlled facilities tailored for agro, pharma, and food supply chains.

Site Development & Infrastructure

Land grading, stormwater drains, internal roads, compound walls, and utility access setup for warehouse zones.

Logistics Support Infrastructure

Docking bays, loading/unloading platforms, office blocks, parking areas, and security cabins.
