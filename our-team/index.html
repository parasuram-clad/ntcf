Dr. Chandramohan K

Chairman

Dr. K. Chandramohan is the visionary Founder and Chairman of NTC Group, who has transformed the company into a global leader in project logistics and diversified infrastructure solutions. Establishing NTC Logistics in 1997, he pioneered the Over Dimensional Cargo (ODC) segment in India and built one of the world’s largest specialized transport fleets.

With his strategic foresight, NTC expanded beyond logistics into infrastructure development through NTC Infra, delivering large-scale projects across civil construction, industrial facilities, and public infrastructure. His ability to align operational excellence with long-term vision has positioned NTC Infra as a trusted partner for sustainable and complex infrastructure projects.

Renowned for his entrepreneurial spirit and leadership, Dr. Chandramohan has received several accolades, including a Lifetime Achievement Award for his outstanding contributions to industry growth. His journey continues to inspire, as he drives NTC Infra with a focus on innovation, quality, and nation-building.

Meet the Team Behind Every Foundation

Mr. Raajasundaram S

Joint Managing Director

Mr. Raajasundaram, Joint Managing Director of NTC Group, is a dynamic business leader who combines financial expertise with strategic foresight. An MBA graduate from Madras University, he has over 25 years of leadership experience guiding the Group’s growth journey across multiple sectors, including infrastructure through NTC Infra.

Since 1997, he has been instrumental in not only managing budgets, treasury, audits, and compliances but also in shaping the overall direction of the Group with a forward-looking vision. His leadership goes beyond numbers—he is known for his ability to build trust, empower teams, and foster collaboration across functions.

Hailing from a family of strong business acumen, Mr. Raajasundaram has developed a leadership style marked by clarity, decisiveness, and people-centric governance. His strong rapport with banks and financial institutions reflects his credibility, while his guidance in infrastructure projects highlights his commitment to sustainable development and long-term impact.

With a balance of strategic insight and hands-on involvement, Mr. Raajasundaram continues to be a driving force behind NTC’s growth, inspiring teams and positioning the Group as a trusted name in the industry.

Mr. Stephenraj M

Mr. Stephenraj is an entrepreneur, strategic thinker, and vision-led professional. His leadership is defined by a powerful blend of courage, discipline, integrity, and resilience — qualities that shape every decision and direction. Impossible is just untried. Vision backed by courage and determined action makes anything achievable — Mr. Stephenraj M

Mr. Muthusamy S

Director – Fleet Operations and Maintenance

Mr. Muthusamy, Director of Fleet Operations and Maintenance, plays a pivotal leadership role in overseeing NTC Logistics’ Namakkal facility. He manages one of the largest and most complex fleets in the industry, with over 2,000 drivers, co-drivers, and operators under his supervision.

Starting his journey in transportation as an entrepreneur with a modest fleet, Mr. Muthusamy quickly built a reputation for operational excellence and a deep understanding of ground-level logistics. His early expertise and vision led him to collaborate with Dr. Chandramohan, where together they laid the foundation for what has today grown into the NTC empire.

Renowned for his hands-on leadership and ability to inspire teams, Mr. Muthusamy ensures that NTC’s fleet operations remain efficient, safe, and reliable. His commitment to discipline, driver welfare, and operational sustainability has been instrumental in positioning NTC as a trusted leader in logistics and fleet management.
