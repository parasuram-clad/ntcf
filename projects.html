Our Projects

Turning vision into reality through engineered excellence across industries.

60 ml/d Sewage Treatment Plant – Transforming Waste into a Resource

NTC Infra has successfully delivered a 60 MLD Sewage Treatment Plant in Perungudi, Chennai, reinforcing its commitment to sustainable infrastructure and environmental stewardship. The plant is designed to treat 60 million litres of sewage per day, transforming wastewater into reusable water for industrial and non-potable applications. Built with a focus on efficiency and environmental compliance, this next-generation facility plays a crucial role in reducing the pollution load on natural water bodies and supporting water reuse in an urban context. By converting waste into a valuable resource, the project highlights NTC Infra’s dedication to building cleaner, smarter cities that are equipped for long-term ecological balance and responsible resource management.

Providing Comprehensive Water Supply Scheme

NTC Infra is implementing a large-scale water supply improvement project in Madipakkam, Chennai, designed to ensure clean, reliable, and sustainable water access for the area’s growing urban population. Central to the project is a high-capacity 50 lakh litre overhead tank and an extensive House Service Connection (HSC) network, enabling direct water delivery to thousands of households. Built with a focus on long-term scalability, efficiency, and environmental responsibility, the initiative not only addresses current water demands but also prepares the region for future urban expansion. This project reinforces NTC Infra’s commitment to strengthening essential civic infrastructure and promoting sustainable urban development.

LB Road Project – Supply and Laying of 900mm Dia CI Main (A) in Area XIV

NTC Infra is undertaking a key sewage infrastructure upgrade in Chennai by replacing the existing 1000mm DI pumping main with two parallel 900mm CI mains (A & B), connecting LB Road Relay SPS to Perungudi SPS. The current phase involves the supply and laying of Main (A) in Area XIV. This project is strategically designed to enhance sewage flow capacity, introduce operational redundancy, and improve the overall reliability of the city's core wastewater system. With a focus on precision, durability, and long-term performance, the initiative reflects NTC Infra’s commitment to building smarter, more resilient urban infrastructure.

45mld Capacity Tertiary Treatment and Reverse Osmosis Plant

NTC Infra contributed to one of Chennai’s most forward-thinking water infrastructure projects—the 45 MLD Tertiary Treatment & Reverse Osmosis (TTRO) Plant at Koyambedu. Built to meet the water demands of rapidly growing SIPCOT industrial clusters, this plant transforms treated wastewater into high-quality process water through an advanced three-stage purification system. From executing civil works for ultra-filtration and RO units to building essential storage, treatment, and support facilities, our team played a critical role in delivering a future-ready solution. Successfully commissioned in November 2019, the project now supplies 45 MLD of reusable water daily, reducing industrial dependence on freshwater sources and marking a significant step toward sustainable urban development.

Windmill Tower Manufacturing Unit

A cutting-edge factory shed is under development in Koyambedu, Chennai, to serve as a specialized windmill tower manufacturing unit. Designed to support large-scale industrial operations, the facility accommodates heavy-duty machinery, precision-driven fabrication processes, and optimized workflow layouts for maximum efficiency. With a strong focus on structural integrity and functional design, this project makes a meaningful contribution to the renewable energy sector by enabling the streamlined, high-volume production of windmill towers. It reflects a forward-looking approach to industrial infrastructure aligned with sustainable development goals.
