60MLD STP at Perungudi

NTC Infra has successfully delivered a 60 Million Liters per Day Modern Sewage Treatment Plant designed for maximum efficiency and environmental sustainability. With advanced treatment systems and a focus on water reuse and energy optimization, this project reflects our commitment to building smarter infrastructure that protects natural resources and supports long-term ecological balance. We incorporated cutting-edge automation, ensuring minimal manual intervention and consistent performance. This project stands as a model for sustainable urban development and responsible engineering practices.

CLIENT CMWSSB
SECTOR Infrastructure
LOCATION Chennai

Water Supply Scheme at Madipakkam

NTC Infra has successfully delivered a transformative water supply improvement scheme for Madipakkam, Chennai, ensuring clean and reliable access for the growing urban population. The project includes 105 km of Ductile Iron Pipeline Network, a 9,000 House Service Connection (HSC) and a higher-capacity 50 lakh liter Overhead Tank — engineered to meet present and future demand. This initiative strengthens community infrastructure while promoting sustainable water management across the region.

CLIENT CMWSSB
SECTOR Infrastructure
LOCATION Chennai

Windmill Tower factory at Melakarandai

NTC Group has successfully delivered a modern factory shed with civil and Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) services in Melakarandai, Tuticorin, to serve as a dedicated Windmill Tower manufacturing unit. This facility is designed to accommodate large-scale fabrication processes, heavy machinery, and streamlined logistics for efficient production. With a focus on structural strength, functional layout, and industrial precision, the project reflects NTC’s commitment to support India’s renewable energy sector through high-performance infrastructure tailored for sustainable manufacturing.

CLIENT Shreevari Energy Systems Pvt Ltd
SECTOR Factory Building
LOCATION Tuticorin

45MLD TTRO at Koyambedu

A key contributor to Chennai’s sustainable water future, this 45 MLD Tertiary Treatment and Reverse Osmosis (TTRO) Plant at Koyambedu supplies high-quality recycled water to SIPCOT industrial clusters. Through advanced three-stage purification, the plant converts treated wastewater into reusable process water. The project reduces industrial reliance on freshwater, with NTC Infra delivering critical civil works, infrastructure for ultra-filtration, RO units, and support facilities.

CLIENT CMWSSB
SECTOR Infrastructure
LOCATION Chennai

900mm Dia CI Pipe Laying at LB Road

NTC Infra has executed the strategic replacement of a 1,000 mm DI sewage pumping main with two parallel 900 mm CI pumping mains (A & B) spanning 6,135 meters from LB Road Relay SPS to Perungudi SPS. This upgrade enhances flow capacity, ensures operational redundancy, and strengthens the reliability of Chennai’s core sewage infrastructure.

CLIENT CMWSSB
SECTOR Infrastructure
LOCATION Chennai

CEO Office at Thiruvannamalai

NTC Infra executed the construction of the Integrated Chief Educational Officer (CEO) Office and Godown Buildings at Thiruvannamalai for the Public Works Department. Designed as the district’s education hub, the facility centralizes governance, planning, and monitoring of initiatives while supporting teacher management and student welfare. With modern office infrastructure and functional storage, the project enhances efficiency and contributes to quality education and student development in the region.

CLIENT Public Works Department
SECTOR Official Building

Knowledge & Study Centre at Namakkal

With modern infrastructure and dedicated spaces, the facility supports independent study, collaborative learning, and professional growth.
